## ğŸ“Œ What is ANOVA?

**ANOVA (Analysis of Variance)** is a statistical method used to:

> Compare the **means of three or more groups**  
> to check whether **at least one group mean is significantly different**.

Instead of comparing means directly, ANOVA compares **variations (variances)**.

## ğŸ¯ Why ANOVA is Used

- To compare **3 or more groups**  
- To avoid multiple t-tests  
- To reduce **Type I error**  
- To study treatment effects
